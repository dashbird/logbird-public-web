Central post-processor for cloud logs.

In modern app development, we use a lot on managed services. In one action, a user can touch tens of different services (like API Gateway, Lambda, Auth0, Aurora, Twilio etc). Logbird processes log streams from all of those services to extract events that you can track and trigger actions.

Endless possibilities, at scale.

Detect application events.

Catch errors and custom events across all services that log out activity. You can choose from a list of community curated filters to get going fast and cover all known services.
