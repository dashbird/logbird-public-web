Not another analytics service.

Logbird is a channel to track and move information using logs. It can scalably process HUGE amounts of logs and extract the meaningful. You can use it to track events from cloud applications, automate workflows or as an event-source to speed up and simplify applications.

Track errors and events across all cloud services that produce logs. Set filters from curated & community-supported list of existing filters or create your own.
